Why do buses bunch up?

Bus bunching happens because, if a bus gets delayed, there are more people than anticipated waiting at the next stop. And the extra time the extra people take to board delays the bus even more. A vicious cycle.

Click play to start the simulation. To delay a bus, hold its bar below. Even a short delay causes bunching.

Hover over a stop to see its history. The area of the curve is cumulative wait time. Bunching makes the area grow.
