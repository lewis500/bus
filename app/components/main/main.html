Why do buses bunch up?

Bus bunching happens because, if a bus gets delayed, more people than anticipated will be waiting at the next stop. The extra time they take to board will delay the bus even more, and so on.

Click play to start the sim. To delay a bus, hold its bar below. Even a short delay causes bunching.

Hover over a stop to see its history. The area of the curve is cumulative wait time. Bunching makes the area grow.
